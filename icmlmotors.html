HISTORY

ICML motors short for “International Cars and Motors Limited” is a subsidiary of the Sonalika Group. The company generally specializes in manufacturing passenger vehicles (SUVs) and commercial vehicles (tractors). As of 2012, the company had reached the fourth spot in the largest tractor manufacturer in terms of sales. The company has newly introduced the ‘Extreme MUV’ which is an advanced version of the famous ‘Rhino MUV’.
