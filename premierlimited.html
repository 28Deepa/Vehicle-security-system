HISTORY

Premier Ltd, previously known as ‘The Premier Automobiles limited’ is a popular automobile manufacturer based in Mumbai, India. The company began its journey into the automotive industry by assembling vehicles of other manufacturers, such as Plymouth and Dodge. Soon, the company began manufacturing the ‘Padmini’ which is perhaps the most popular taxi all around India. Now, the company even manufactures passenger vehicles, machine tools, and buses.
