ASHOK LEYLAND LIMITED

  • Years: 1948 – Present
  • Headquarters: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India
  • Founder/Parent Company: Hinduja Group
  • Website: http://www.ashokleyland.com/

HISTORY

Based in Chennai, Ashok Leyland is among the leading companies for commercial vehicles in India. The company doesn’t actually manufacture passenger vehicles, but is popular for producing some of the best commercial vehicles. It is the 2nd largest commercial vehicle manufacturer in India and has six operating plants across India. The company sells approximately 7,000 engines and 80,000 commercial vehicles annually and is the leader in the bus segment.
