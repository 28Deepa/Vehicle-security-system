INDIAN CAR COMPANIES

Formerly known as ‘Tata Engineering and Locomotive Company (TELCO), Tata Motors is one of the leading Indian car companies. The company was founded way back in 1945 and was first a manufacturer of locomotives, having introduced its first commercial vehicle in 1954. The automotive industry is headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, and has numerous assembly and manufacturing plants.
Formerly known as Maruti Udyog Limited, Maruti Suzuki is one of the most popular Indian car manufacturers. It is a subsidiary of the popular Japanese motorcycle and automobile brand, Suzuki. The vehicle company is known for its reputation for producing some of the most budget-friendly vehicles. It was founded in 1981 and manufactured its first vehicle in 1984. It has more than 933 dealerships across 666 cities and towns in India with 3,060 service stations around 1,454 towns and cities and 30 Express Service Stations around 30 National Highways.
Hindustan Motors was founded way back in 1942 and is an automotive manufacturer that is based in Kolkata, West Bengal of India. The company was the most popular and largest car brand in India until 1980 when Maruti Suzuki took its place by producing some of the most fuel-efficient and budget-friendly vehicles. Hindustan Motors is generally the producer of the famous Indian Ambassador motorcar, which was inspired by the design of Morris Oxford. Most of the politicians in India still travel around in Ambassadors.
Based in Chennai, Ashok Leyland is among the leading companies for commercial vehicles in India. The company doesn’t actually manufacture passenger vehicles, but is popular for producing some of the best commercial vehicles. It is the 2nd largest commercial vehicle manufacturer in India and has six operating plants across India. The company sells approximately 7,000 engines and 80,000 commercial vehicles annually and is the leader in the bus segment.
Headquartered in Maharashtra, Mahindra & Mahindra Limited is in Indian multination car manufacturer. It is by far the largest manufacturer and seller of tractors across the world and is one of the biggest vehicle manufacturers in India. The company is a part of the Mahindra Group and is in constant competition with Tata Motors, Maruti Suzuki, and Ashkok Leyland.
Opel India Private Limited is a division of the General Motors India Private Limited. The company has been producing vehicles since 1996, but since the arrival of Chevrolet in India, the Opel brand has now discontinued manufacturing vehicles. For now, the company only provides spare parts to existing vehicle owners. Some of the most famous cars manufactured by Opel include the Vectra C, Astra F, and of course, the Corsa B.
The Eicher Motors Limited is an automobile manufacturer based in Gurgaon, India, that mainly specializes in producing commercial vehicles, such as buses, trucks, and tractors. The company’s history dates back to 1948 where it began its journey as a tractor company. However, it officially became an automobile manufacturer in 1982 and even produces bicycles, motorcycles, automotive gear and other components.
Bajaj Auto Limited is one of the most popular automobile manufacturers in India. The company first started off selling motorcycles and three-wheelers and soon began its way up to the manufacturing of cars. Bajaj is the world’s third-largest manufacturer of motorcycles and three-wheelers and is known for its reputation of manufacturing low-cost cars. Just recently, the company has introduced a compact car known as the RE60 which is highly fuel-efficient and budget-friendly.
Premier Ltd, previously known as ‘The Premier Automobiles limited’ is a popular automobile manufacturer based in Mumbai, India. The company began its journey into the automotive industry by assembling vehicles of other manufacturers, such as Plymouth and Dodge. Soon, the company began manufacturing the ‘Padmini’ which is perhaps the most popular taxi all around India. Now, the company even manufactures passenger vehicles, machine tools, and buses.
Force Motors, previously known as ‘Bajaj Tempo’, is one of the most popular manufacturers of heavy commercial vehicles, buses, tractors, light commercial vehicles, cross-country vehicles, and three-wheelers. The company has changed its named twice and was originally named after N.K Firodia, but after joining hands with Bajaj Auto, became ‘Bajaj Tempo’. Some of the most popular vehicles produced by Force Motors include the Traveller (Ambulance), Tempo Matador, Mindor, Trax Judo, and One.
ICML motors short for “International Cars and Motors Limited” is a subsidiary of the Sonalika Group. The company generally specializes in manufacturing passenger vehicles (SUVs) and commercial vehicles (tractors). As of 2012, the company had reached the fourth spot in the largest tractor manufacturer in terms of sales. The company has newly introduced the ‘Extreme MUV’ which is an advanced version of the famous ‘Rhino MUV’.
