HISTORY

Opel India Private Limited is a division of the General Motors India Private Limited. The company has been producing vehicles since 1996, but since the arrival of Chevrolet in India, the Opel brand has now discontinued manufacturing vehicles. For now, the company only provides spare parts to existing vehicle owners. Some of the most famous cars manufactured by Opel include the Vectra C, Astra F, and of course, the Corsa B.
